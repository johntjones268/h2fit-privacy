H2FIT Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

H2FIT ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: Name, email address, password (stored as a secure hash), military rank, grade, unit, and MOS.
  • Personal information: Date of birth and gender, used solely to calculate age and gender-appropriate Army Fitness Test (AFT) scores.
  • Workout data: Workout type, duration, distance, and GPS route coordinates recorded during activity tracking.
  • AFT scores: Army Fitness Test results you manually enter.
  • Location data: GPS coordinates collected only during active workout recording. We do not track your location in the background.
  • Heart rate data: With your permission, H2FIT may read heart rate data from Apple Health during workouts to display average heart rate alongside your activity. This data is never accessed outside of an active workout session.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide workout tracking and AFT scoring features
  • To display your activity in the in-app social feed visible to other H2FIT users
  • To calculate age and gender-appropriate AFT performance scores
  • To maintain your account and authenticate you securely

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your workout data (name, workout type, distance, duration, and GPS route) is visible to other H2FIT users in the app feed. You can delete your account at any time from the Profile tab, which permanently removes your data from our systems.

Data Security

Passwords are stored using bcrypt hashing and are never stored in plain text. Authentication uses industry-standard JWT tokens. All data is transmitted over HTTPS.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time through the app's Profile tab, which will permanently remove your personal information from our servers.

Children's Privacy

H2FIT is intended for use by adults (18 and older). We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
